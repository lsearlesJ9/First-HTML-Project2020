My Biography

Larry Searles

Larry Odis Curll Searles was born and raised in Southern California. He spent time between the city of San Diego and Riverside County, California.

He grew up participating in numerous sports from Soccer, Football, Basketball, and Baseball.

He was a standout track athlete in highschool while also playing for his high school Varisty Football Team.

He enlisted into the US Army immediatley after high school. He was sent overseas during his time with the US Army.

He currently works as a Senior Project Coordinator for a small firm and is pursuing a career in the software industry.
